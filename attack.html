About the attacks

Deauth (Disconnect Devices)

This stops a device from staying connected to WiFi by sending a special signal that tells it to disconnect.Example: Imagine kicking someone off a Zoom call by sending a fake “disconnect” message that’s what this does to WiFi.

Beacon (Fake Networks)

This creates fake WiFi networks around you by sending out WiFi names.Example: If you add names like “Free_WiFi” or “HackNet” to the list, they’ll show up in other people’s WiFi menus but they won’t actually connect to anything.

Probe (Fake Searches)

This makes the device act like it’s searching for saved WiFi networks, even if they’re not nearby.Example: It’s like walking around asking, “Is Starbucks_WiFi here?” over and over — it confuses trackers that try to follow your device.
